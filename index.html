About the Maker

Craft rooted in
honesty & precision

Every piece that leaves the shop is made from carefully selected hardwoods — walnut, white oak, maple — chosen for character and longevity. No shortcuts, no veneered particleboard. Just real wood, real joinery, real furniture.

Based in Portland and building for clients throughout Oregon and beyond. Whether it's a heirloom dining table, a custom bathroom vanity, or a unique storage cabinet, the process starts with a conversation and ends with something you'll pass down.
