About the Maker

My name is Mark Phillips, founder and creator of MP Woodworking. Raised in Northern California, I began woodworking early and spent more than 20 years honing my craft in the trades, specializing in fine cabinetry for luxury residential homes.

What started as making cutting boards and frames for friends and family evolved into designing and building custom furniture and bespoke pieces. I was trained to build with longevity and precision — quality over quantity is my guiding principle. Tell me your vision and I'll bring it to life with craftsmanship you can trust.
